To the Woman I Hurt

This website is not here to erase what I did. It is here because I want to take full responsibility for the pain I caused you.

I Am Sorry

I know that my actions hurt you deeply, and I want to say this clearly: I am truly sorry.

Not just because things became difficult, and not just because I am afraid of losing you, but because I know I caused pain to someone who means so much to me.

You did not deserve to be hurt by me.

I Take Responsibility

I do not want to hide behind excuses or make my mistake sound smaller than it really is.

What I did was wrong. I understand that apologies mean little if they are not honest, and honesty means admitting fully that I failed you in a way that cut deep.

I own that. Completely.

I Understand Your Pain

I know that pain like this is not just about one moment. It can affect trust, peace of mind, and the way you look at everything that came before it.

I understand that what happened may have made you feel disappointed, disrespected, confused, or emotionally tired. And knowing that I caused those feelings in you breaks me.

What I Am Learning

I am learning that love is not only about affection, words, or good intentions. It is also about discipline, honesty, and protecting the heart of the person you love.

I failed in that, and I do not want to stay the same person who caused you this pain.

What I Want You to Know

I am not asking you to ignore your pain. I am not asking you to forgive me right away. I am not asking you to pretend everything is okay.

I only want to be truthful with you and let you know that I see the weight of what I did.

And if there is ever any chance for me to rebuild what I broke, I know it has to be through change, consistency, and patience.

No Pressure, Only Honesty

Whatever you feel right now is valid. Whatever time you need is valid.

This message is not meant to pressure you. It is simply my heart trying to be accountable, because you deserve truth, respect, and sincerity from me.

With remorse and love,
Mark

Made with sincerity, not excuses.
